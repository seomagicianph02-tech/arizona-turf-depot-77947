Frequently Asked Questions About Artificial Turf Installation

How much does artificial turf installation cost in the Phoenix area?
At Arizona Turf Depot, pricing for artificial turf installation typically ranges from $8 to $14 per square foot, depending on the turf style, project size, and site preparation needed. For a more accurate estimate, we offer free on-site consultations in the Phoenix area to assess your yard, discuss your needs, and provide a detailed quote.
How long does it take to install artificial grass at my home in Phoenix?
Most residential installations in the Phoenix area are completed within 1 to 3 days, depending on the size and complexity of your yard. Factors like access to the installation area, removal of existing grass, and any custom features (like putting greens or pet areas) can affect the timeline. We'll provide a specific schedule during your consultation.
Is Arizona Turf Depot licensed and insured to perform turf installations in Arizona?
Yes, Arizona Turf Depot is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for artificial turf installation in Arizona. Our ROC license number is available upon request, and we adhere to all local and state regulations to ensure your project is done safely and professionally.
What is the installation process for artificial turf in the Phoenix climate?
Our installation process is tailored for the Phoenix climate. We start by removing existing grass and debris, then install a weed barrier and a compacted crushed rock base for proper drainage. Next, we lay and secure the artificial turf, infill it with a material suited for high heat (such as antimicrobial sand), and groom the surface. We also account for the intense Arizona sun by recommending UV-resistant turf products.
Do you offer any warranty or guarantee on your turf installations?
We stand behind our work with a 2-year installation warranty that covers workmanship and installation defects. Additionally, the artificial turf products we use include manufacturer warranties of 8 to 15 years against fading and material defects. We'll provide all warranty information in writing at the completion of your project.
